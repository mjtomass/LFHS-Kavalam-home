Little Flower High School Kavalam.

Little Flower HS Kavalam, located at Kunnumma is one of the popular schools in India.It is an aided High School in Kuttanadu DEO under the Corporate Management of Archdocese of Changanacherry. This institution is counted among the top-rated Schools in Kerala with an excellent academic track record.The school started its 'Bon Voyage' as a lower primary school in 1927.Upgraded to UP school later and uplifted again to High School in 1984.

  • Established on 1927.
  • Upgraded to HighSchool in 1984.
